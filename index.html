Vill-V

Helical Contraption

Vill-V is a member of Fire Moth and the owner of Helixcraft. She is ranked 5th among the Thirteen Flame-Chasers, her codename being "Helix".

Vill-V also has a habit of delegating each persona specific tasks and thus specific knowledge, which makes it seem as though she has multiple personalities.

Vill-V has the ability to split her mind in two. In theory, she could do so infinitely, but her brain power would be spread thin. As of now she has split her mind into 8 parts.

Appearance

Vill-V is a young woman with medium length dual-toned hair, split in the middle between light and dark brown. Her eyes are grey with bright pink pupils.
